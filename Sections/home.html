I hold a PhD in English Literature from NUML Islamabad, with a specialization in postcolonial and poststructuralist studies. I have over two decades of experience in teaching, research, and curriculum development, and have authored several academic publications and books. As a passionate educator and mentor, I strive to foster critical thinking, literary sensibility, and research excellence among my students.
